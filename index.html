How it works

Follow the below instructions to achieve the best results.

Before you conduct the experiment, make sure that:

  • Your headset device is charged and connected to its application.
  • You are sitting in a quite room with no distractions.
  • You follow the intrsuctions as shown in the experiment.

Before you upload your data to the model, make sure that:

  • Your data file has the extension .csv

Before you play the focus game, make sure that:

  • Your headset is connected.
  • You are broadcasting from Emotiv.
  • You pay attention

Credibility

Our expirment is designed based on approved reasearch criteria. The expirement contains 2 tasks, each are known to be standard tasks in the evaluation of ADHD.

Our model uses deep learning techniques to carry out the classification process. It has been trained on over 8000 data files and scored a testing accuracy of 80%.

Our game uses mental commands to move the game character. This was achived by training the game on these specific commands by setting up a training profile.
